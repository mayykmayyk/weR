who wer

WeR Solutions Inc. provides exceptional remote staff for global companies at a very competitive cost.

Our highly educated workforce, based out of the Philippines, is driven by a strong work ethic, an unparalleled focus on customer service and possessing refined natural English language skills.

Beyond the traditional administrative services, WeR also provides Social Media Management, Content Writing/Moderation, IT and Web Services Support, and others.

WeR is a startup remote support company built on our founders’ background and experience from the different industries they have been immersed in.

WeR focuses on providing administrative and customer support services while being guided by the principle that to uphold our business, we need to uphold our clients' dignity, respect, and trust and provide our service with patience and competence.

MISSION

WeR’s mission is to help enhance our clients' businesses by providing secure and reliable remote (VA) support with an unparalleled service level designed around each client’s unique needs.

VISION

WeR’s vision is to establish a trusted global platform that makes the Company synonymous with premium remote (VA) support services.

VALUES

WeR guided by the principle that to uphold our business, we need to:

  1. Uphold the dignity, respect and trust of our clients in their most frustrating moments
  2. Provide our service with patience, compassion, and competence.

our management team

SOPHIE BAUTISTA-SALES

CO-FOUNDER
MANAGING DIRECTOR

Sophie has spent the last ten years in the customer service industry, working in call centers for well-known companies like the Intercontinental Hotels Group (IHG) and eBay (UK and North America).

Sophie embarked on several jobs as a result of her expertise in call center operations, eventually rising to the position of Head of HR for an Australian IT firm based in the Philippines. She oversaw an indirect workforce of 200 people working in online design/development, web/mobile app development, and customer service.

Sophie was asked to join a startup VA company as Vice President of Operations and Head of HR after a few years. Sophie was a key contributor to and manager of the company's expansion from 6 to 60 employees in under two years.

frequently asked questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the services we offer:

  • What kind of services do you offer?

    We provide Social Media Management, Content Writing/Moderation, IT and Web Services Support, to name a few.

  • Do you cater to all business sizes?

    Yes. We provide our services to small, mid-sized, and large businesses.

  • How long will WeR spend on a project?

    The total time to complete a project depends on what type of services our clients want from us and how soon they need us to get things done. No matter what, we finish our tasks on or before the agreed deadline.

  • Are all my information secure?

    Yes. WeR considers all information about your account private and takes precautions to safeguard it.

    We use a multi-tiered security system that includes IP limitations, screen monitoring software, and safe data encryption. All personnel who handle customer information must sign non-disclosure agreements and get data security training.

  • How much are WeR’s services?

    Our service pricing usually depends on the tasks you want us to take on. Reach out to our team to get a quote.
