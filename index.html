how we do it

STEP 1: WeR the Right Fit

Connect with WeR Consultants so we can help you explore your processes to pinpoint what you have, what you need, and what can be improved to determine if we are the right fit. If WeR fits, we will work out an agreement for you to sign before we move on to the next step.

STEP 2: Meet our Busy Bees

WeR Consultants will work with you to take a more in-depth look into your needs, reviewing the tasks and tools along with the culture and personality of your company to find the best matching WeR VA for you.

STEP 3: Getting into the Hub

Now that we know how WeR can help, our team will interview, sift, and select the best talents to work with you. We have a pool of competent, trustworthy, and compassionate VAs who are ready to help you achieve your goals. From data encoders to project managers, we can source highly-qualified individuals with excellent command of the English language to take on tasks crucial for your business.

STEP 4: WeR Ready

Here is where it all begins! After assembling the best VA team for you, you will meet your assigned WeR talents and the consultant to set your process, tools, and goals as you head together towards success.

STEP 5: Staying on Track

Your WeR Consultant will be close by during the first few weeks of the partnership to assist you and your talents as you adjust to working together. For the staff who will be assigned to you, there will be a dedicated point person to monitor your staff’s performance, manage your and their concerns proactively, and provide updates on your assigned staff’s progress. The WeR team will regularly check-in to make sure that everything stays on track.
